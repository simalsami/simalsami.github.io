About Me...

Hello! I am currently in my fourth year pursuing a major in Information Technology with a minor in Computer Science, specializing in Integrated Data Analysis at the esteemed University of Scranton. As a woman of color, I take pride in my journey and aspire to be a trailblazer in the tech industry. Upon entering college, I discovered the profound value of effective leadership, sparking a keen interest in integrating leadership principles into both my academic and professional pursuits. This passion has propelled me to assume leadership roles within my campus community, complementing my rigorous curriculum and dedicated involvement in undergraduate research. Motivated by the transformative power of technology and its profound impact on our global landscape, I have directed my focus toward a fulfilling career in the cybersecurity field. My ultimate aim is to ascend to technical leadership roles, driven by a deep-seated enthusiasm for the ever-evolving nature of technology and its potential to shape our world. As I approach graduation, I am actively seeking full-time opportunities that align with my passion for cybersecurity and leadership.
